Pan-Seared Salmon

Description

Pan-Seared Salmon is a simple way of cooking a salmon fillet.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Half a lemon
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • Herbs such as dill or parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season defrosted salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Gently place salmon in pan.
  3. Cook for 4-5 minutes on either side until brown crust forms. Reduce heat to medium-low.
  4. Add minced garlic and let saute before topping salmon with it.
  5. Once deepest point in salmon has reached 120 farenheit, take out and let rest under tin foil.
  6. Season with herbs and the juice of a lemon. Serve.
